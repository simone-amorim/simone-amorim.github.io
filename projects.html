Projects


1. SOPLE - Service-Oriented Product Lines Engineering
(Edital CAPES/DGU - Brasil/Espanha n: 022/2011)

The goal of the SOPLE project is to develop an integrated development concepts of software product lines and service-orientation process. Thus, the following activities should be undertaken:
- Search the state of the art in the field of software product lines oriented services.
- Search state of the art in modeling and management of variability.
- Definition of the product lines of software process service-oriented phases including scoping, requirements, architectural design.
- Definition of controlled experiments to evaluate the process.
- Definition of case studies with companies to evaluate the process.
An important aspect is that while the Brazilian team is focused on the phases and stages of the process, the Spanish team is interested in how usability aspects can be entered and managed along the proposed process.

Participant as PhD student.


2.SPLSmart - Development of a Software product Line for Medical Management
(Edital RHAE/CNPq Nº 062/2009 - R$ 298.069,71)

The objective of this project is to develop a software product line of products for the medical management applications (office, clinic, laboratory and hospital) and later re-development of these products.

Participant as PhD student.


3. AADSP - An adaptive approach to deployment of software development processes in micro and small companies in Salvador/BA
(Editais nº 04 and 05/2014/PRPGI- IFBA and Edital nº 01/2015/PRPGI – 2015/2016 - IFBA/FAPESB)

This project proposes and evaluates an adaptive approach to deploying software development process in micro and small companies. It applies innovative practices, agile methods and an ecosystem-based platform based on usable components for software products. It also makes possible to improve the quality and productivity. The evaluation approach will be carried out through case studies in software developers companies in Salvador/BA.

Participant as Researcher.
